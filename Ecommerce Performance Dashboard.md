
### **📌 Project: E‑commerce Performance Dashboard (Power BI)**  
A fully interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyse sales, customer behaviour, and product performance for an online retail store. The dashboard provides end‑to‑end visibility across revenue trends, customer segments, product categories, and operational KPIs.  

---

### **🎯 Objective**  
To build a data‑driven analytics solution that helps business stakeholders quickly identify performance patterns, uncover growth opportunities, and make informed decisions using real‑time insights.

---

### **📊 Key Features & Visuals**
- **Sales Trend Analysis** — Line chart showing revenue performance over time, highlighting seasonality and growth patterns.  
- **Top Products & Categories** — Bar charts ranking best‑selling items and categories by revenue and quantity sold.  
- **Customer Insights** — Segmentation by new vs returning customers, demographics, and purchase frequency.  
- **Profitability Metrics** — KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, Average Order Value, and Conversion Rate.  
- **Geographical Performance** — Map visual showing regional sales distribution.  
- **Interactive Filters** — Date slicers, category filters, and customer segment selectors for dynamic exploration.

### **🧠 Insights Delivered**
- Identified **peak sales periods** and seasonal demand patterns.  
- Highlighted **high‑performing product categories** driving majority of revenue.  
- Revealed **customer retention gaps**, showing opportunities to improve repeat purchases.  
- Exposed **regional performance variations**, enabling targeted marketing strategies.  
- Demonstrated **profit concentration**, helping prioritise high‑margin products.

---

### **🔧 Tools & Techniques**
- Power BI Desktop  
- Data Modelling (Star Schema)  
- Power Query for data cleaning and transformation  
- Interactive report design using bookmarks, tooltips, and drill‑through pages

---

### **📈 Impact**
This dashboard provides a **single source of truth** for business leaders, enabling faster decision‑making and improving visibility across sales, customers, and product performance. It demonstrates strong capability in **data modelling, visual analytics, and business insight generation** — key skills for Business Analyst and Data Analyst roles.
